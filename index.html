About these buttons

The font and style matches that of gmail, what you see here is the official Google Product Font. You can use your browser's render settings to adapt font size to your liking and you cana render this page as PDF to have them in vector format.

These are some of the replies that Gmail's system has suggested over the last year. The first thing that struck me about them is how upbeat and affirmative they all are. These of course are only in English language, but in the very few emails that I still exchange in Spanish the suggestions for Gmail's algorithm were also in Spanish. Perhaps there's another work there as well, since it is likely that the algorithm preserves this slightly creepy upbeat tone across languages. Anyway, this is a start. You can render this page as a PDF for vector graphics. I am sure that many German speakers get a "Wunderbar!" button in their suggestions as well.
